The DS Lab

The DS Lab is an academic-focused learning channel dedicated to Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. Created for students who want to learn in a structured, concept-first manner without shortcuts or hype.

Intended for learners enrolled in formal AI programs, including those offered by IITs. It is also relevant for students aiming for GATE DA, higher studies, research, and academically oriented internships.

The channel focuses on building strong foundations in Python, mathematics, statistics, and machine learning. Run by a student pursuing formal academic training in Data Science as a mentor-style learning space.
